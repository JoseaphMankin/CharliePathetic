Welcome to the Charlie Pathetic Homepage.

Laramie Wyoming's Premier Ska Core

This site serves as a memorial to Charlie Pathetic, a Ska Band based in Laramie WY.

While only active for a short time (1998-2000), CP played a spattering of shows, recorded a full length album at the Sonic Iquana in Layfette Indiana, and appeared a few compilation CDs
